Hojicha Cookie Recipe

Use this recipe to create a batch of soft, chewy hojica cookies!

Ingredients:

  • 190g (1 1/3 cups) all-purpose flour
  • 2.5g (1/2 teaspoon) baking powder
  • 3g (1/2 teaspoon) baking soda
  • 0.75g (1/8 teaspoon) salt
  • 10g (2 tablespoons) hojicha powder
  • 60g (1/4 cup) granulated sugar
  • 120g (3/4 cup) brown sugar
  • 90mL (6 tablespoons) neutral oil(vegatable oil)
  • 60mL (1/4 cup) milk
  • 5mL (1 teaspoon) vanilla extract
  • 100g (1/2 cup)chocolate chips

Steps:

  1. Stir together 190 g (1 1/3 cups) flour, 2.5 g (1/2 teaspoon) baking powder, 3 g (1/2 teaspoon) baking soda, and 0.75 g (1/8 teaspoon) salt in a medium bowl. Set aside.
  2. Combine 10 g (2 tablespoons) Hojicha Powder, 60 g (1/4 cup) granulated sugar, and 120 g (3/4 cup) brown sugar in a large bowl.
  3. Add 90 ml (6 tablespoons) oil and stir, then add 60 ml (1/4 cup) milk and 5 ml (1 teaspoon) vanilla. Mix together until well incorporated.
  4. Sift in flour mixture, and stir until just combined.
  5. Fold in 100 g (1/2 cup) chocolate chips.
  6. Cover and chill the dough in the fridge overnight (or a minimum of one hour).
  7. When ready to bake, preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C) and line two baking sheets with parchment paper.
  8. Scoop or roll 12 balls of cookie dough and place on the prepared baking sheets. Leave about 2 inches between the cookies.
  9. Chill each batch of cookie dough for 10 minutes before baking.
  10. Bake for 10 minutes until the edges begin to set and the middle is slightly under baked.
  11. Let the cookies cool on the baking sheet for 10 minutes.
  12. Gently transfer the cookies onto a wire rack to full cool and firm up.
  13. Serve your freshly baked hojicha chocolate chip cookies with a hojicha latte!
