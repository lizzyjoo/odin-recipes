Hojicha Latte Recipe

Use this recipe to make a glass of ice-cold, rich Hojicha latte!

Ingredients:

  • 5g Hojicha powder
  • 1 part (60mL) 80Â°C water
  • 3 part (180mL) milk (Oat milk is recommended)
  • (optional) ice cubes

Steps:

  1. Whisk the hojicha powder in warm water.
  2. Add milk and whisk more until it is homogenous.
  3. Serve in a cute glass with some ice cubes!
