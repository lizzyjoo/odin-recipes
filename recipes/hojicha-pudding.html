Hojicha Pudding Recipe

Use this recipe to make jiggly Hojicha pudding!

Ingredients:

  • 1.5 teaspoons Hojicha powder
  • 1 tablespoon tapioca starch powder (60mL) 80Â°C water
  • 1.5 tablespoons sugar
  • pinch of salt
  • 20mL cold water
  • 100mL milk
  • 1/4 tablespoon vanilla extact

Steps:

  1. Add 1.5 teaspoons of hojicha powder, 1 tablespoon of tapioca powder starch, 1.5 of tablespoons sugar, and a pinch of salt into a small saucepan. Mix well until no white streaks remain.
  2. Add 20 ml of cold water, and mix until the dry ingredients are fully dissolved and form a thick paste.
  3. Add 100 ml of your choice of milk and stir well.
  4. Heat the mixture over medium heat, while stirring occasionally.
  5. As soon as the mixture begins to steam, lower the heat to medium low. Then stir continuously while making sure to scrape the bottom.
  6. Remove from heat right before the mixture reaches your desired firmness, as the pudding will continue to thicken as it cools.
  7. Stir in 1/4 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  8. Pour into a jar and refrigerate.
  9. Serve your hojicha pudding chilled or at room temperature.
